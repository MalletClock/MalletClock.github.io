About the project

This project was made while taking part in a game jam. It is a game about a dog with a very important mission of bringing music back to his world. I got to experiment with sprite animations and sound design. You can find the game here!


My role

I created the main character, Lennu. Using sprite sheets, I tried to give some life to Lennu through animations. In addition I created the sound and music used in the game, using recordings from a electronic keyboard.
